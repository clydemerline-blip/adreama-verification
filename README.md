
Adreama Turtle Habitat Program (ATHP)

Overview

The Adreama Turtle Habitat Program (ATHP) is a private, non-commercial habitat initiative based in Florida. The program is focused on the responsible care, maintenance, and observation of legally acquired, non-breeding turtles and tortoises.

Purpose

ATHP exists to:

- Provide a safe and controlled habitat environment
- Ensure proper care and welfare of all animals
- Maintain accurate records of health, feeding, and habitat conditions
- Operate in alignment with Florida wildlife guidelines

Animal Status

All turtles and tortoises within ATHP:

- Were obtained through legal commercial sources
- Are not used for breeding purposes
- Are maintained solely for private habitat and observation

Compliance Intent

ATHP is committed to operating in full compliance with all applicable Florida regulations. This repository serves as a transparent record of the program’s structure, intent, and operational standards.

Documentation Included

- Habitat setup details
- Care procedures and routines
- Health tracking and record-keeping systems
- Program descriptions for review and verification
